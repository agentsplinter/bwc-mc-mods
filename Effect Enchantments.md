Effect Enchantments is a mod that adds a unique twist to your Minecraft gameplay by turning all possible potion effects into enchantments for weapons and armor! Now, you can enchant your gear with powerful effects like speed, regeneration, and many more giving you more control over your combat and survival strategies.

**What Makes It Unique:**
- The only mod that offers cross-version compatibility with separate versions for each Minecraft release (currently a work in progress).

**Features:**
- Effects as enchantments for Weapons and Armor

- Perfect for enhancing combat, exploration, and utility
