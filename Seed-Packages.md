🌾 Seed Storage Simplified!

Tired of chests overflowing with random seeds? Wish there was an easier and cleaner way to organize them all? Look no further! This mod introduces a simple yet powerful solution for managing every kind of seed in your world.

With this mod, you can:

🌱 Store any seed whether it’s from vanilla Minecraft or another mod in a single compact item

📦 Seal your seed container to create a tidy, bulk storage option that saves space and reduces clutter

🌾 Plant directly from the item, making it perfect for both organized farmers and casual players alike

🪴 Keep your inventory clean and efficient, no more stacks of loose seeds taking up space!

Whether you’re running a massive automated farm or just want a neater way to store your garden essentials, this mod keeps your seeds organized, accessible, and ready to plant whenever you are.

Say goodbye to seed chaos and hello to streamlined farming!
